    About

    A non-partisan voter information tool built to compare policy alignment, not brand recognition.

    What it is

    Scot Vote Manifesto Matcher is a voter information tool for the 2026 Scottish Parliament election. It lets you choose policy topics, set your position on each, and see which parties align most closely with your priorities.

    It is designed to keep the interface neutral, the scoring method visible, and the underlying source status clear when manifesto data is still pending.

    What it is not

    It is not an endorsement tool, a voting recommendation engine, or a prediction model. It does not attempt to tell users who to vote for.

    It also does not hide its scoring formula. You can review the full calculation logic on the Methodology page.

    How the data works

    Party manifesto data files provide the current best-source metadata, including whether the source is a published manifesto or a website-based estimate. Topic scores are then read from the fixed score tables in the shared runtime so the matching logic is consistent across the site.

    When a party publishes a manifesto, its manifesto data can be updated and the site will reflect that change on the next deployment. If you spot something that needs correction, use the Contact page.
