    Other Parties

    These parties are not included in the matcher, but they may still be of interest if you want to explore a wider range of parties standing or seeking to stand in the 2026 Scottish Parliament election.

    This page is a reference list only. It does not affect your match result and it is not intended to be a complete ballot database.

    Scottish Family Party

    Leader: Richard Lucas

    Website: scottishfamily.org

    Key issues: family policy, parental rights, school standards, tighter immigration, free speech, opposition to abortion and euthanasia, and opposition to gender recognition reform.

    Scottish Socialist Party

    Leader: Colin Fox and Natalie Reid

    Website: scottishsocialistparty.org

    Key issues: socialism, public ownership, council housing, independence, anti-poverty measures, workers’ rights, public transport, and anti-war politics.

    Scottish Christian Party

    Leader: Dr Donald Boyd

    Website: ukchristianparty.org

    Key issues: Christian values in public life, opposition to abortion, freedom of speech, socially conservative policy, marriage, and support for the Union.

    Animal Welfare Party

    Leader: Vanessa Hudson

    Website: animalwelfareparty.org

    Key issues: animal protection, biodiversity, climate action, plant-based diets, ending harmful farming practices, and stronger animal welfare law.

    Libertarian Party

    Leader: Alex Zychowski

    Website: libertarianpartyuk.com

    Key issues: lower taxes, smaller government, free markets, individual liberty, deregulation, and limits on state intervention.

    Scottish Common Party

    Leader: No single leader listed on the official website

    Website: scottishcommonparty.org.uk

    Key issues: strong public services, affordable housing, safer communities, fair work, lower bills, and practical local reform.
